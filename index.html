Services

What We Do

Core Hardware Diagnostics & Repair

  • The Baseline DiagnosticBench testing to pinpoint component failures (PSUs, RAM, storage, fans) when a system is unstable or won't post.
  • Storage Modernization (Mechanical to SSD)Swapping slow HDDs for high-speed SSDs, including a complete clone of the client's existing drive or a flat-rate "Clean Slate" OS installation.
  • Component & Capacity UpgradesPhysical installation of expanded RAM, replacement power supplies, fresh cooling fans, or dead CMOS batteries.
  • Logical Data RecoveryPulling data from a non-posting or corrupted machine and transferring it safely to an external drive.

Infrastructure Design & Building

  • Network Mapping & DocumentationCreating professional, visual topology maps of existing small-office layouts.
  • Server Closet Cleanup & Structured CablingTrimming out messy "spaghetti" wiring, mounting hardware racks, and organizing patch panels.
  • Small-Office Wi-Fi & Network DeploymentsTransitioning environments from consumer routers to managed, centralized ecosystems (like Ubiquiti UniFi or TP-Link Omada) with secure VLAN segmentation.

Custom PC Sourcing & Assembly

  • The Component Architecture BlueprintConsulting on budget and performance goals to deliver up to 3 tailored part options (Budget, Sweet Spot, Premium).
  • Turnkey AssemblyBuilding the system from shipped/dropped-off parts, managing cables, flashing the stable BIOS, and running baseline stress tests.
